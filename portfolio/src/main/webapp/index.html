A Bit About Me

Hello! My name is Jessica Feng, and I am a sophomore at Cornell University, majoring in Computer Science. I'm fascinated by the flexibility and creativeness of programming, and hope to explore many avenues of tech while learning about the diverse impact computer science has on the world. When I'm not typing away on my computer, you can catch me bopping to good music, watering the plants in my mini-garden, or capturing nature's beauty through photography.

How I Got Here

I've always been awestruck by the vast potential computer science has and its global impact. The interdisciplinarity and versatility of the field allows for the perfect amount of breadth and depth into various topics seemingly unrelated to technology. As someone who wants to explore all of her interests, I felt computer science would be a fitting path for me to take, with just the right amount of individual creativity mixed with team collaboration and technical diversity.

My photography!

I started my photography journey from the desire to preserve memories, whether it be on my elementary school field trip or on my way to class. The camera acts as my alternate vision, where I see everyday happenings in a different light and want to save that portrayal of my feelings in that moment. Photography allows me to lose track of time and diverts my focus from the screen to appreciating and capturing a moment in time.
